TLDR - kill
==========

Overview
--------
[kill] - to terminate process


Examples
--------
 uses *signals* 
	kill -*signal* 

 signal types: 
	SIGTERM -15 (terminates process in orderly manner) 
	SIGINIT -2 (interrupt process but can be ignored) 
	SIGKILL -9 (interrput the process cannot be ignored)
