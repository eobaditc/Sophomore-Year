TLDR - tar
==========

Overview
--------

an archiving utility used to compress and decompress files with the .tar extension

Examples
--------

- decompress archive (with .gz)

    $tar -xvzf *file*

- ompress archive to *path* and name it *name*

    $tar -cvzf data.tar.gz /*path*/*name*
