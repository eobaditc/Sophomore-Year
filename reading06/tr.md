TLDR - tr
=========

Overview
--------

translate or delete characters from stdin and writes to stdout

Examples
--------

- change from lower to upper 

    $tr 'a-z' 'A-Z'

- elete characters and do not translate

    $tr -d ' ' (gets ride of white space)
